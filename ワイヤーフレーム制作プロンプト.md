High-fidelity long-scroll website UI wireframe for a Japanese business service named "「可視化経営」実践会" (Visualization Management Practice Session). Target audience: SME owners in their 40s-50s.

[**IMPORTANT DESIGN INSTRUCTION**]
- **COLOR PALETTE:** Deep Royal Blue and rich Champagne Gold with metallic gradient accents. Background is clean white (except for the Risk section). Premium, trustworthy executive feel.
- **LAYOUT:** **Strictly Vertical Storytelling.** DO NOT use cramped multi-column grids. Stack elements vertically with generous whitespace to ensure easy reading while scrolling.
